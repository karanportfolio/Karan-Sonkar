Project Overview

Nissim India Pvt. Ltd. is an established radiator manufacturer with over 30 years of industry experience, producing heavy-duty cooling solutions for tractors, trucks, earth-moving equipment, and industrial generators. The project required transforming a traditional manufacturing business into a strong digital brand capable of attracting high-value B2B clients and OEMs.

The Client's Goal

To strengthen online presence, generate targeted B2B business enquiries, create professional corporate branding, and effectively promote their extensive range of industrial radiators through social media and Meta Ads.

The Hurdles

Key Challenges

Niche Audience

Industrial products require highly specific targeting, making generic consumer marketing ineffective.

Technical Visuals

Creating attractive, premium social media creatives for highly technical, heavy-duty machinery parts.

B2B Trust Building

Establishing brand credibility to attract cautious B2B buyers, distributors, and OEMs.

Platform Fit

Adapting traditional manufacturing communications for dynamic platforms like Instagram and Facebook.
