Project Overview

CameraKart is a premier online platform in India dedicated to the buying and selling of new and used cameras. The business recognized the need to dominate search engine results to capture high-intent users actively looking to sell or buy camera equipment.

The Client's Goal

To dramatically improve Google visibility, increase organic traffic, rank for highly competitive commercial keywords, and generate a steady stream of high-intent leads through data-driven SEO.

The Hurdles

Key Challenges

Low Keyword Visibility

Struggling to rank on the first page for core transactional search terms.

Technical SEO Issues

Website architecture and crawlability needed significant technical refinement.

Weak Page Optimization

Existing content and internal linking lacked clear commercial search intent.

Performance Drops

Website speed and image optimization were hindering user experience and rankings.
